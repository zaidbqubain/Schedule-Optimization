Student-Department Feedback

Communication between students and department faculty provides a way to improve the curriculum, in general. Updating assignment time estimates and providing general feedback for faculty, by assignment, enables faculty with information about how students work.

  • Students will be able to give feedback on a faculty-provided time estimate for each assignment
  • Faculty can use responses to reformat or shorten assignments to provide a better learning environment
